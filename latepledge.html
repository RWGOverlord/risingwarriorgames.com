How to Play

A Turn in Five Steps

Each round is fast, aggressive, and full of decisions. Here's how a turn unfolds.

1
Gain Resources & Buy Cards

Play Supply cards to build up Purchase Power, then spend it to buy new Traps, Action cards, or Scavengers from the shared Wasteland.

2
Build Your Base & Activate Abilities

Place Trap cards into your base to set up defenses and lure types. Activate their special abilities to gain an edge — more resources, card draws, or disruption.

3
Command Your Horde

Play Minion cards to attack rival bases. Hit their Life Points directly, or break and deactivate their Traps to disrupt their lure strategy before they can use it against you.

4
Lure & Convert Survivors

Survivors in the Wasteland are drawn to whoever has the most matching resources across their active Traps. If you lure them, they flip into Zombie Minions and join your deck permanently.

5
Clean Up & Draw New Cards

Discard all unused cards from your hand and draw back up. Your newly converted zombies shuffle into your deck, making every turn stronger than the last.

Become the Overlord

Last player standing with Life Points wins. Out-lure, out-build, and out-battle your rivals to claim your rotten throne.
